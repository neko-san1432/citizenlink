How It Works

Our platform simplifies the complaint process for both citizens and government units.

For Citizens

  1. 1

    Create an Account

    Sign up with your email and create a secure password.

  2. 2

    Submit a Complaint

    Fill out the complaint form with details about your issue.

  3. 3

    Track Progress

    Monitor the status of your complaint through your dashboard.

  4. 4

    Receive Resolution

    Get notified when your complaint is resolved by the government unit.

For Government Units

  1. 1

    Access Admin Dashboard

    Log in to the LGU platform with your credentials.

  2. 2

    Review Complaints

    View and manage incoming complaints assigned to your unit.

  3. 3

    Assign Resources

    Delegate complaints to the appropriate department or personnel.

  4. 4

    Monitor Performance

    Track resolution metrics and improve service delivery.
